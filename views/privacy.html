Privacy Policy

1. Data collected

We collect account email, encrypted password hashes, reservation details, and contact form submissions.

2. Purpose of processing

Data is used to authenticate users, provide booking features, support requests, and improve service quality.

3. Security controls

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Session cookies use HttpOnly settings and secure transport in production.

4. Data retention

Information is stored only as long as needed for account, booking, and support operations.
